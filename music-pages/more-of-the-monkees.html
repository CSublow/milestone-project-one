More Of The Monkees

Release year: 1967

1967's More Of The Monkees was the band's top seller, racking up 70 weeks on Billboard's album chart, including 18 weeks at #1, becoming the 3rd best-selling LP of the '60s (ranking higher than any Beatles album). The Monkees topped the pop singles charts again with the Neil Diamond-penned "I'm A Believer."
